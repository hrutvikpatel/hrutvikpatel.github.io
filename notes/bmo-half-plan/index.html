    # BMO Half Marathon plan (May 3 2026)

    VDOT is roughly 34 based on the best-effort races (5K 26:03, 10K 62:26, etc.). Wednesday and Saturday remain the only training days, so the plan leans on those two workouts to carry load.

    # Pace zones (min/km)

    • Easy / Recovery: 6:30‑7:05 (RPE 3‑4) – used for optional midweek recovery walks/slow jogs and Saturday long runs when volume is lower.
    • Long run: 6:15‑6:55 (RPE 4, toward the faster side later in the plan). Keep these conversational even if the total is high.
    • Tempo / Threshold: 5:05‑5:40 (RPE 7‑8). Start at the slower end (5:35‑5:40) and finish around 5:05‑5:15 closer to race day.
    • Intervals / VO₂ max: 4:40‑5:10 (RPE 8.5‑9.5). Shorter workouts lean toward the fast end; longer interval-based sessions may sit near 5:00‑5:10.
    • Race pace (Half): 5:40‑5:55 (target race pace predicted from the Runna estimate of 1:59‑2:07).
